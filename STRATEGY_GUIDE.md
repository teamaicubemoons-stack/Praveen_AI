# 📊 EMA Pro V12 - Strategy Guide

This guide explains how to master the **EMA Pro V12 (Supreme Hybrid)** strategy for maximum profitability and safety.

## ⚙️ Core Logic
The strategy uses a triple EMA crossover system combined with RSI and ATR filters to identify high-probability trend continuation trades.

*   **Fast EMA (21)**: Captures short-term momentum.
*   **Slow EMA (50)**: Confirms the medium-term trend.
*   **Trend EMA (200)**: Ensures we only trade in the direction of the "Big Trend".

## 🛡️ Risk Management (The Secret Sauce)
Professional trading is about NOT losing money. This strategy includes:

1.  **Risk per Trade (1.5%)**: Automatically calculates position size so you never lose more than 1.5% of your equity.
2.  **Partial Profit (TP1)**: Closes 50% of the trade at 1.5 ATR profit to lock in gains.
3.  **Break-Even (BE)**: Once TP1 is hit, the remaining 50% stop loss moves to the entry price. **Risk becomes ZERO.**
4.  **Final TP (TP2)**: A wide 3.5 ATR target to capture massive "Moonshot" moves.
